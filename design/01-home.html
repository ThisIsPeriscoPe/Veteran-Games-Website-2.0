Why The Veteran Games Matter

More Than A Competition

The Veteran Games is a proactive health initiative addressing key findings from the Royal Commission into Defence and Veteran Suicide. Connection, purpose and identity, delivered through competition.

1,500+Veterans lost to suicide in two decades
