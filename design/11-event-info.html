The Veteran Games National Finals are Australia's premier military inspired team competition for Veterans. Across two days, teams from around the country compete in demanding physical and tactical challenges that showcase purpose, community, service and excellence, while bringing veterans, families and the public together. Every event is scored on Time, Technique and Teamwork.

Get Ready

Participation Requirements

  • A competitor may only register to compete in one team and must be current or former serving military personnel.
  • Each team elects one Team Captain responsible for registration, check in and communications during the Games.
  • Competitors and Reserves may be substituted through the Team Captain with event staff prior to an activity.
  • Competition time slots are allocated post registration.

Clothing

  • Camouflage pants, your choice
  • Boots or shoes, your choice
  • T shirt or team shirt, your choice

Training Tips

  • Stretcher carry and stores carry, 100 to 200m
  • Rope climb and descent
  • Swimming 50m in clothes
  • 20kg to 50kg equipment carriage, solo and in pairs
  • Obstacle course fundamentals, walls and cargo nets
  • Knots
  • Basic first aid, tourniquet, splints and bandages
  • Tyre flipping technique
  • Shuttle runs and short sprints
Good To Know

FAQ, Insurance & Cancellation

Any person who has served within the Military is eligible to compete in the Veteran Games. Competitors may be former or current serving personnel, Australian or overseas.

Teams are 8 competitors plus 2 reserves. The Team Captain registers the team and team name first, then manages check in and communications on the weekend. The Finals are capped at 25 teams.

We have obtained Public Liability coverage to ensure the safety and protection of all attendees. All stalls and vendors must hold their own Public Liability insurance and provide proof of coverage prior to the event. We also recommend personal insurance to protect against unforeseen accidents and travel insurance for interstate teams.

If for some reason, including Force Majeure, the Veteran Games must be cancelled, all competitors and supporters will be notified immediately. Registrations automatically carry forward to the following year and full refunds will be provided to any competitors that choose to withdraw as a result of the cancellation.

Every activity is scored on Time, Technique and Teamwork. The team with the strongest combined performance across the weekend takes the title.
