Course Experience Overview

What You Are In For

Competing in teams of 8 plus 2 reserves, you take on the Obstacle Course Challenge and surprise events built on problem solving and teamwork. Each event replicates activities conducted during active military service.

Every activity is scored on Time, Technique and Teamwork. Train for all three. The course rewards the team that moves as one, not the team with the fastest individual.

Obstacle 01

Run Dodge Jump

Flat out from the whistle. Sprint, react and clear whatever the course throws in your path. Pure speed under pressure and the crowd favourite opener.

Obstacle 02

The Ninja Wall

Sheer, tall and unforgiving. Nobody clears the Ninja Wall alone. Boost, pull and haul every team member over the top. This one is won with trust.

Obstacle 03

Tug of War

One rope. Two teams. No hiding. The oldest test of collective grit there is, and it still settles arguments the honest way.

Obstacle 04

Medical Stand

Tourniquets, splints and bandages under the clock. The skills that kept your mates alive in service still count here. Precision beats panic.

Obstacle 05

Casualty Evac

Stretcher up, casualty on, move. Weight, distance and fatigue stack against you. Leave no one behind is not a slogan here. It is the event.
